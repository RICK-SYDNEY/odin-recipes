WORLD'S BEST PIZZA

Pizza is a beloved dish worldwide, known for its versatility and delicious flavors. This recipe will guide you through making a classic homemade pizza from scratch.

How to Make Pizza

Making pizza at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. Below are the basic steps to prepare a delicious pizza:

Pizza Ingredients

Ingredients to have before we start are as follows:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella is traditional)
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs and spices (oregano, basil, garlic powder)

The key to a great pizza lies in the quality of the dough and the freshness of the toppings. Feel free to customize your pizza with your favorite ingredients!

STEPS

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 500°F or 260°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your desired toppings evenly across the pizza.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil and sprinkle herbs and spices on top.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
